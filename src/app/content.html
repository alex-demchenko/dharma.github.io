Buddhism

Buddhism is all traditions of transmission, understanding, practice of dharma.

1. Dharma

Dharma is teaching of liberation from dukkha.

1.1. 3 jewels

3 jewels are buddha, dharma, sangha.

1.1.1. A person with great merit

A person with great merit become buddha or cakravartin.

1.1.1.1. Buddhist

Buddhist is a person who practice buddhism.

1.1.1.1.1. Lama

Lama is tibetian buddhist teacher.

1.1.1.1.1.1. Ole Nydahl

Ole Nydahl is lama.

1.1.1.1.1.2. Garchen Rinpoche

Garchen Rinpoche is lama.

1.1.1.1.2. Thich Nhat Hanh

Thich Nhat Hanh is buddhist monk.

1.1.1.2. Cakravartin

Cakravartin is wheel-turning monarch.

1.2. Abhidharma

Abhidharma is the highest dharma.

1.3. Dukkha

Dukkha is all kinds of unpleasent vedana.

Path leading to cessation of dukkha is noble eightfold path.

Conditions for occurrence of mental dukkha are mental object, clinging.

1.3.1. Vedana

3 types of vedana are pleasent vedana, neutral vedana, unpleasent vedana.

1.3.1.1. 5 aggregates of clinging

5 aggregates of clinging are rupa, vedana, samjna, sankhara, vijnana.

1.3.1.1.1. Rupa

Rupa is form.

1.3.1.1.2. Conditions for occurrence of mental dukkha

Conditions for occurrence of mental dukkha are mental object, clinging.

1.3.2. Path

Path leading to cessation of dukkha is noble eightfold path.

Noble eightfold path is right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right samadhi.

4 stages on path to awakening are sotapanna, sakadagami, anagami, arhat.

1.3.2.1. 4 noble truths

4 noble truths are there is dukkha there is origin of dukkha there is cessation of dukkha there is path leading to cessation of dukkha.

1.3.2.1.1. Cessation of dukkha

Path leading to cessation of dukkha is noble eightfold path.

1.3.2.2. Right speech

Right speech is no lying, no abusive speech.

1.3.2.3. Right samadhi

Right samadhi, right immersion, right concentration are synonyms.

1.3.2.4. Sotapanna

Sotapanna is stream-enterer.

1.3.2.5. Sakadagami

Sakadagami is once-returner.

1.3.2.6. Anagami

Anagami is non-returner.

2. Practice

Informal practice, daily-life practice are synonyms.
